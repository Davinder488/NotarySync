    Davinder Singh Heera, Notary Public & Loan Signing Agent

    As a dedicated and reliable Notary Signing Agent, I am committed to providing exceptional loan signing services. I am NNA-certified, E&O insured, and a member of the NNA. With extensive training from the NNA Notary Essentials course and the Loan Signing System, I am well-prepared to handle a wide range of loan signing assignments.

    I prioritize accuracy and efficiency in every aspect of my work. I meticulously review all documents to ensure they are complete and error-free before proceeding with notarizations, stamps, initials, and signatures. My goal is to provide a seamless and positive experience for all parties involved, including borrowers, title/escrow companies, loan officers, and realtors.
